Our Teams

Our teams are comprised of three sub units:

  • Baby Team
  • Toddler Team
  • Pro Team

They are separated based on skill level and have ranging commitment hours per week.

Baby Team

The Baby Team has one main piece they practice throughout the semester. The average time commitment for the Baby Team is about 3-5 hours per week. This includes all practices, technique meetings, and personal practices members can do on their own.

Toddler Team

The Toddler Team has three pieces they practice throughout the semester. The average time commitment for the Toddler Team is about 5-7 hours per week. This includes all practices, technique meetings, and personal practices members can do on their own.

Pro Team

The Pro Team has five pieces they practice throughout the semester. The average time commitment for the Pro Team is about 6-8 hours per week. This includes all practices, technique meetings, and personal practices members can do on their own.

How to Join

Baby Team

Everyone is initially added into the Baby Team but can go through auditions to graduate to a higher level. In order to join the Baby Team, you need to go to two open practices before submitting an application. While it is not required, going to at least one of our Coffee Chats will raise your chances of being accepted as we more becoming more selective. And then, submit a Google Form Application!


Toddler and Pro Team

All you need to do is audition! Come to an additional practice before auditions and you will be placed into one of our teams based on your skill level.
