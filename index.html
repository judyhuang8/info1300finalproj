Mission Statement

We are a team of motivated, creative, and talented dancers who strive to create a welcoming and supportive community on campus through our passion for dance. We are inclusive, welcoming, and willing to accept anyone who is willing to learn. We teach dances to our subteams of various dance experience, as well as host performances and showcases to share out love for dance to the Cornell community.

General Info

We are an on-campus organization at Cornell University that is comprised of undergraduate and graduate dancers. We accept dancers with no experience as well as experienced dancers. Our dancers are separated into subteams of various previous dance experience, where they learn and practice techniques and learn new movements.
